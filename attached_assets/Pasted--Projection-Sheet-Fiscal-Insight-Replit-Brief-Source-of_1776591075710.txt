## Appendix — Quick Reference Formulas

```
Team total / resource / year       = CTC × Months × (Alloc % / 100)
Team total annual                  = Σ resources
Overhead / line / month SAR        = USD × FX rate
Overhead / line / year SAR         = Monthly SAR × 12
MS cost / resource / year          = CTC × Months × (Involvement % / 100)
Core cost / client / month         = (Team annual + Overhead annual) / Clients / 12
MS cost / client / month           = MS annual / 12              (NOT / Clients — MS is per-client team)
Sell ex-VAT                        = Cost / (1 − Margin %)
Sell inc-VAT                       = Sell ex-VAT × (1 + VAT %)
Invoice #1 inc-VAT                 = Setup inc-VAT + Infra inc-VAT + Core inc-VAT + MS inc-VAT
Invoice #N (N = 2..12) inc-VAT     = Core inc-VAT + MS inc-VAT
Year-1 total / client inc-VAT      = Invoice #1 + 11 × Invoice #N
Year-1 profit / client             = Invoice #1 profit + 11 × Invoice #N profit
```

> ❗ Note on Managed Services allocation: Excel's 121,800/yr is the cost to staff the MS pod for ONE client's worth of service (PM at 5%, SA at 30%, Engineer at 100%). If you're serving 5 clients, you need 5× that pod — so MS annual cost = 121,800 × Clients = 609,000, and MS cost / client / month = 121,800 / 12 = 10,150. Don't divide by Clients a second time.
